We like to talk about stuff

Originally from California and I've had one hell of a ride in my 35 years thus far. Went from a troublemaker who didn't care about graduating high school to a proud sailor in the U.S. Navy, to a Sheriff's Deputy, to an Athletic Trainer for the San Francisco 49ers. Currently trying to break down the door in the tech industry to become a web developer. In the meantime I'm molding young minds as a substitute teacher.

I enjoy drawing & painting, playing golf and recently fell in love with improv comedy.

Spent my early years on the east coast and moved to Ventura as a wee lad. Was a band geek in high school and didn't have friends so I joined the Navy. Did my time, got out, got married (so now I'm doing a different kind of time), went into graphic arts and transitioned to web development.

I play the drums, enjoy golfing even though I suck, and my board game collection is ever evolving.
